About US

Lifestyle diseases are common among the population today not only in India but also in almost every country. Lifestyle diseases are caused because of the habits that we have on a day to day basis. The way one lives his life is the major cause of it. It includes heart disease, hypertension, chronic kidney disease and liver disease, etc., which all may hear of. In our life also, one also comes across at least one person who is either suffering from such diseases or the diseases became the reason for his death. We also came across many such people who died because they were not aware of their disease and were left with no appropriate time for treatment. That is why we decided to develop the portal which will analyse the data entered by the user and will give the predictions of the diseases. which he or she may have chances to suffer from. In this project, we developed an application for Chronic Kidney Disease, Cardio Vascular Disease and Liver Disease prediction using Machine Learning. This project makes the person aware of his health so that he will have the treatment well in time if required and will save the lives of many people. In this, we used a different machine learning models to find prediction of disease like CKD, Heart disease & liver disease & demonstrated with the help of Web application. Lifestyle diseases include atherosclerosis, heart disease, and stroke; obesity and type 2 diabetes; and diseases associated with smoking and alcohol and drug abuse.

Aim and Objectives

Aim

The primary aim is to develop predictive models that can assess the risk of lifestyle diseases in individuals based on various parameters such as diet,
exercise habits, medical history, genetics, and environmental factors.

Objectives

The objectives of lifestyle disease prediction using machine learning typically revolve around leveraging advanced computational techniques to anticipate the likelihood of an individual developing certain health conditions based on their lifestyle choices and other relevant factors. Here's a breakdown:

  • Early Detection: Detect lifestyle diseases at an early stage when they are more manageable.
  • Personalized Risk Assessment: Provide personalized risk assessments to individuals.
  • Preventive Interventions: Implement preventive interventions and lifestyle modifications tailored.
  • Data Analysis and Selection: Analyze large datasets containing information on lifestyle habits.
